"Hyper what now?"

Yeah! Hypertext Markup Language! It's rad! and it's the skeletal

headers, refrences, links and images that every site needs to work.

"ol.. ul... ulol"

One of the best coolist parts of HTML is lists!

There are unordered lists and ordered lists!

There's ordered lists where you can make cronological lists

  1. 1st place
  2. 2nd place
  3. 3rd place

And unordered list where you can go wild! Or uh... mundane

  • Soy milk
  • Sugar free cereal
  • A more exciting lifestyle

You can even do LISTS WITHIN LISTS!

  • List of the best lists ever

    2. college supplies
    3. this list
  • List of the worst list ever
    1. vegan groceries lists

    3. this terrible list
